Our Story

At The Cozy Cat, we believe in the magic of purrs and warm companionship. Nestled in the heart of Stillwater, MN, our cat café offers a welcoming escape where you can sip your favorite coffee or tea while enjoying the playful charm of our friendly feline residents. Each of our cats is a rescue with a unique story, and we’re proud to partner with local shelters to help them find their forever homes. Whether you're here for a cozy moment or to meet your new best friend, The Cozy Cat is the purr-fect place to relax, connect, and unwind.
